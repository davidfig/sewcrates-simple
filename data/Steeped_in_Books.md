I can't wait to read and write and doodle and not work.</p><p class="normal">I'm fighting off a cold that is threatening to ruin my book vacation. I'd hate to get to Taipei and be turned away at the airport for fear of flu.</p><p class="normal">We have our Naginata seminar and test this weekend.
