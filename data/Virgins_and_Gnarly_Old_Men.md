<p class="normal">Of gray talons and glued ears rising off the pillowed earth;<br>For bright clips and yellow drops falling off the mother’s breast;<br>And rainbowed hues dodging green blues for what has been spoken reels.</p><p class="normal">To crazy horses and forever zippers sliding down spokes of gold,<br>Over purple houses and greasy raisins coming across melting streets;<br>Where footprints of old and canes of new forever dripping over smiles.</p><p class="normal">And where downy feathers, with bloody black scabs stabbing cardinal skin,<br>Rise over nights of chocolate glaze and trials of cola dying to be born free,<br>Sound off the cadence of late, leading virgins and gnarly old men home.</p>
