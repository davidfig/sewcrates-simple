<p class="normal">Zox and blast and cartoon pigs<br>and trumpets and fries lathered with pink bow ties,</p><p class="normal">to singing frogs and drums and large floppy feet<br>covered by foamy soap spread on a chocolate rope</p><p class="normal">where grueling clocks and tired beds and leaky cheeks<br>find pasty flies sleeping on golden baked ryes;</p><p class="normal">seventy noses and lashes and webbed amigos<br>lie soundly awake and frown down at the milk-fed town;</p><p class="normal">And clothed and zippered and green pickled truth<br>screech the lazy call of understanding and Socks committed crazy.</p>
