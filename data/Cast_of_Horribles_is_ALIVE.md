<p class="normal">After a few weeks of work, I am proud to announce the launch of my new home for <a href="http://castofhorribles.com" title="Cast of Horribles">Cast of Horribles</a>.</p><p class="floatRight"><a href="http://castofhorribles.com" title="Cast of Horribles Website screenshot"><img src="/assets/misc/1300_COH.JPG" alt="Cast of Horribles Website screenshot"></a></p><p class="normal">The coding was rather fun, as I moved away from flat files, and delivered all the content (including the doodles) through MySQL. I've used databases in the past, but not much. After programming the site, I realized how much of an idiot I was for not using a database for sewcrates. I think the revelation was almost at the level of my coffee awakening.</p><p class="normal">Along with the doodles, I'll also try to post short, pithy musings. They won't have the consternations or length of a sewcrates post, but they should be more frequent. I will continue to ply away at storytelling and longer musings on sewcrates, as well as uploading new photographs and keeping my about page more current with my list of <a href="/books/" title="books">books</a> and <a href="/movies" title="movies">movies</a>--although, to be fair, my movies aren't updated as often since I've stopped buying DVDs and rent everything through Netflix.</p><p class="normal">I will continue to post the newest doodle on the front page of sewcrates, but have removed it from the RSS feed for annoying technical reasons (use the <a href="http://castofhorribles.com/rss.xml" title="Cast of Horribles RSS feed">Cast of Horribles RSS feed</a>).</p><p class="normal">Enjoy, and let me know how much you like/dislike the new site! I'm always up for suggestions. And as soon as I receive the expected <a href="http://liminality.org" title="Chuck">Chuck</a> e-mail, explaining how the design is terrible and unintuitive and downright ugly, I'll cry for a bit, before yanking it down and hopefully making it better. I've resolved myself to this course of action even before I post this.</p><p class="normal">Enough consternated self-loathing. (It wouldn't be a real sewcrates post without them!)</p>
