<p class="normal">Today's work was the result of my posting <a href="http://castofhorribles.com/Life_changes_when_people_drop_in_to_stay/" title="Life changes when people drop in to stay.">this doodle</a> about a month before our wedding. My mother immediately called:</p><p class="normal">"David."</p><p class="normal">"Yes, mom?"</p><p class="normal">"Is there something you're not telling me."</p><p class="normal">"There's lots I'm not telling you."</p><p class="normal">"I'm talking about your drawing yesterday, the one with the parachute. Is Doolies, you know?"</p><p class="normal">"Pink?"</p><p class="normal">"No, pregnant?"</p><p class="normal">"Huh?"</p><p class="normal">"Well, you drew someone dropping in a parachute, and I was thinking you might be trying to tell us something--you know, subconsciously."</p><p class="normal">I failed, clearly. After all this time, nobody has a clue who's who. For the record, I'm the beige one, Doolies is the pink one, and the blue guy is the philosopher, a combination of my friends. Doolies was dropping in on my life. I decided to draw this picture as way of explanation.</p><p class="normal">So, thanks to that conversation, I bring you the ultimate guide to the Cast of Horribles, pre-marriage and pre-Ziggy.</p>
