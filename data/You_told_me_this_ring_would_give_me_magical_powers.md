<p class="normal">When we were looking for rings, I told Doolies that after the wedding I didn't want to wear the ring. I decided to give the ring a chance only if it provided me with powers. I told her I would accept invisibility or invulnerability, but actually I was open to anything.</p><p class="normal">As I was sitting around with Chuck and Charles after the Taiwan wedding, we were discussing what powers my  ring granted me. I forget which one said it, but they told me that my ring did not grant me powers, but granted Doolies powers.</p><p class="normal">I do wear my ring more than not, but we'll see how long it lasts. I occasionally attempt my "Sleeeep" (think Dark City) technique, or try to move objects with my ring-clad hand, but so far no good. I'll keep you updated on the powers of the ring.</p>
