<p class="nospaceLeft">Stomach tubes far behind left juices churning in throat opening void.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">As air rises and below land falls, mortality question for obedient muscle’s falter.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Swimming feet in lakes of slimy residue and legs quiver</p><p class="nospaceLeft">thanking muscles of goo, one foot over the other to chant inspiringly vigor.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">As thoughts and memories coalesce, and danger and weakness are forefront brought,</p><p class="nospaceLeft">memories of falls and jumping fantasies overcome the sensible foe.</p><br>
