<p class="nospaceLeft">four skies of liquid grain</p><p class="nospaceLeft">fall upon the soldered earth</p><p class="nospaceLeft">on top of speakers dripping rain</p><p class="nospaceLeft">and holding back their merry mirth</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">poor rain falls up shards of glass</p><p class="nospaceLeft">rising, dropping forming on bows</p><p class="nospaceLeft">and like the gold bathed on the mass</p><p class="nospaceLeft">finds frozen drops hailing their foes</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">tour fabrics douse the crowd's fear</p><p class="nospaceLeft">with remains of crates and crushed seeds</p><p class="nospaceLeft">upon the mouths of a broken tear</p><p class="nospaceLeft">falls the knell of forgotten needs</p><br>
