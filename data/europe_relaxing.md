<p class="normal">It’s amazing how different people find relaxation through different means. After 3 days of various relaxing activities, it took playing basketball today for three hours for me to finally fully relax and feel good. A little swim in the late afternoon in the crystal clear ocean after the weather cleared up, followed by a 2 hour nap in the shade next to the Jacuzzi and then basketball, and now I finally feel fully relaxed. Not sure how long it’ll last, but I can always hope it’ll last through the next 3 weeks.</p>
