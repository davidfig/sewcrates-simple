<p class="nospaceLeft">Gray clouds covered blackened heavens.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Ratchets cracked.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Pop, they released fires through the sky.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Heavens blazed.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Acrid smells reached down to the crowds.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Drums swelled.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Cheers cried over the screams of delight.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Hearts thumped.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Gnarled fingers pointed to cover their ears.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Necks craned.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Trumpet explosions rocked painted hills.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Whistles pierced.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Ancient music built to the sitting ovation.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Legs cramped.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Mammoth sparkles filled the traipsing images.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Breathing stopped.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Brightness flashed through rocks explosions.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Crowds erupted.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Creased flags waved the smallest children.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Dreams sparkled.</p>
