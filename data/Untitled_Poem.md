<p class="normal">Daggers of piercing blind my eye<br>for a dark taloned blade that catches my heart<br>and all that's left within my mind<br>but a semblance of truth and an empty cry.</p><p class="normal">A forest of black within me roar<br>with an unanswered cry of a solemn oath<br>and all that's left within my mind<br>is a sparkling thought of a wing's silent soar.</p><p class="normal">And fires of pain that burn my face<br>that rise above my aching need<br>and all that's left within my mind<br>is a grief less spoken and frozen in space.</p><p class="normal">For the thorn that forms within our veins,<br>which forever keeps us broken apart<br>and all that's left within my mind<br>is the memory of our battles and thoughts and pains.</p>
