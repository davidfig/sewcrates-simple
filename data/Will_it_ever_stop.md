<p class="normal">Ah, rain in Seattle. The summers in Seattle are supposed to be rain-free. That's what they tell you before you move here. On average, Seattle has no more rain than NYC. The average is skewed because of the summers. If we take the median instead of the average, I bet that number changes significantly.</p><p class="normal">The mornings are growing colder and darker. Dragging myself out of bed these mornings is growing harder and harder.</p>
