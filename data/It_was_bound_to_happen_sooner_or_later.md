<p class="normal">This is the opposite of timely. It's been so long, I almost forgot the name of this <a href="http://www.warhammeronline.com/" title="game">game</a>. We stopped playing a few months ago. It was fun while it lasted--lots of keeling other players and buying new weapons and armor. We settled into familiar roles: Doolies, the brawny fighter rushing to the front. David, the lightly armored healer/mage hiding in the back and trying to keep Doolies from keeling herself too quickly. The memories.</p>
