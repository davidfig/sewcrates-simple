<p class="normal">The Watchmen did not suck. We were very happy to see that. The movie could have lost thirty minutes and been a bit better (for the mainstream), but we don't understand the negative reviews. It was both faithful to the comic book, and enjoyable as a standalone movie. I think the end threw people off. The characters in the movie are not good and evil. There's a healthy spectrum that violates most moviegoers expectations.</p>
