<p class="normal">I'm very tired. To catch up on my lost sleep, I need to sleep in. When I sleep in I wake with a headache. This is a vicious problem.</p><p class="normal">Doolies and I went out to dinner last week. This was one of those bad days where my head was pounding. We walked back to the parking lot to drive home. I opened the door to a gray Prius and slid in. It felt different, but I didn't know how different until two women approached. "What are you doing in our car," they asked. We got out, apologized, and found our gray Prius two spots away. This can only happen in Seattle.</p>
