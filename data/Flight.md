<p class="nospaceLeft">Dreams are cast over a pillowy view</p><p class="nospaceLeft">as eye’s sparklies dance across the royal blue glow.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Like leaves caught in a current the movers move</p><p class="nospaceLeft">soaring through space to where lift fails the feathered folk.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Miles over home and heading toward the light,</p><p class="nospaceLeft">unbounded highways lead the way</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Over patchwork quilts of green and blue,</p><p class="nospaceLeft">to mystery and joy over horizons of night and unspoiled dawn.</p><br>
