<p class="normal">I'm looking at you, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Californication_(TV_series)" title="Californication">Californication</a>! We watched the first season of this show, but I vetoed watching more episodes because of the annoying situations the characters found themselves in. Yes, I know terrible things are going to happen when everyone finds out the truth. Just please, for the love of everything television worthy, don't stretch out the period between when the audience finds out and when the characters do.</p><p class="normal">This was a nice, fun-filled weekend. The clocks jumped forward, and Doolies and I both passed our Naginata exams. It's almost sunny this morning, and I expect good things this week. Good things.</p>
