<p class="nospaceCenter">Of millions of Doolies flitting across my moments, it’s those without which remain.</p><p class="nospaceCenter">Moments of holding hands while raisins rise and leaves reach past shadows,</p><p class="nospaceCenter">The truffle oil smell of hair while I rest chin on head;</p><p class="nospaceCenter">The smile so large I lose days falling through it;</p><p class="nospaceCenter">Squeezes so tight I bruise feathered pillows forgetting she’s gone.</p><p class="nospaceCenter">I share crazy thoughts and inhibitions and wait for her to turn the spigot and end the dream.</p><p class="nospaceCenter">Reddened eyes of fondness grow fonder at distances so great they disrupt solitary ambitions.</p><p class="nospaceCenter">I dream of holding her moistened hand and raising her arm with each swinging stride.</p>
