She'll keep Ziggy company when we're away, and hopefully provide Doolies with a portable warmer as Ziggy prefers to cuddle next to me when we watch our evening movies. I tried to explain to her that I couldn't do better than a curvy line and two eyeballs when I started, and Ziggy joined us at a better time, but she doesn't buy it. If Button joins the family (we're fostering her before adopting to ensure she fits in), then I'll have to draw two dogs.
