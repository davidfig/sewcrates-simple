<p class="nospaceLeft">March, my friend. March.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Through the flood of people jigging to the dance of coins.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">March and stop and look and jig.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Plead, beg, dismiss, and haggle.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">March, my friend. March.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Through the market of fleas.</p><br>
