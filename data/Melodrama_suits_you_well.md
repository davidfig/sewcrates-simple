<p class="normal">Life is about emotion. Write about what you know. Write about life.</p><p class="normal">The setup. The conflict. The emotion. The fall.</p><p class="normal">So much more that needs to be said before I get there. Characters the reader needs to fall in love with, then the knife, the retched wound. That’s what I want to share. How do I share it? How do I say?</p><p class="normal">Zealotry is for whiners.</p><p class="normal">She lived. She fell in love. He fell in love. They loved. She died. Anguish. Angst.</p><p class="normal">Zealously.</p>
