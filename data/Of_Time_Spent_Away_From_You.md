<p class="nospaceLeft">Tree limbs dipped to find you,</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Plucked from racing streams;</p><p class="nospaceLeft">I doubted an elfin woman would capture me,</p><p class="nospaceLeft">I long questioned the sway of all women.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">And yet, evidence of perseverance thrives</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Encasing and lifting me from my bemused shape.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Moons pass overhead as heavens rise,</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Illuminating my solid mask, it fractures.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Green daffodils surround us presently,</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Sands and suns boil a salmon's call.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Field's littered, corrupted by space, await us,</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Stumbling and raising hair for summer.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Sliding across an icy rhythm we wait;</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Descending across tears dried crusty brown;</p><p class="nospaceLeft">What becomes of our travails?</p><p class="nospaceLeft">The same can be said of our distance.</p><br>
