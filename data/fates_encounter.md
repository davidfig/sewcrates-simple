<p class="nospaceLeft">On a night as blue as a forever rain,</p><p class="nospaceLeft">spinning winds of fate in a web spotted drops,</p><p class="nospaceLeft">walks a beauty drawn chiseled force and damaging bearing.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Not a beat of heart or a blink of eye passes</p><p class="nospaceLeft">before his tumbling heart falls before her sculpted feet.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">As his blood ceases to pump and</p><p class="nospaceLeft">fields of cotton sprout on his tongue</p><p class="nospaceLeft">their eyes meet like in a choreographed show</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">All of time waits the darkening sky,</p><p class="nospaceLeft">the lady judges and weighs—</p><p class="nospaceLeft">he smiles and casts his wager as hope’s spring splashes his quivering lips</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">As the coin flies through the air and</p><p class="nospaceLeft">fate’s web slowly coalesces,</p><p class="nospaceLeft">another dream passes before the crooning sky.</p><br>
