<p class="normal">the Clown lives for eternity;<br>at least until the end of man.<br>He watches the follies of life,<br>judging with an unlawful Hand.</p><p class="normal">His beconing is hard to stop,<br>all must come to His call.<br>Although the wheel may slow His voice,<br>nothing can stop its awful roll.</p><p class="normal">after the call what is there left<br>but an emptiness and a void?<br>is it really all that dark there,<br>a place that all should strive to avoid?</p><p class="normal">but under all the Clown's makeup,<br>could it be that there is some peace?</p><p class="normal">a place in which the soul of man<br>is saved from its unlawful hand.<br>should We decide when to enter<br>the clown's domain, or wait for HIS soft call.</p><p class="normal"><span class="italics">(Forgotten Date)</span></p>
