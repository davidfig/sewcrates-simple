<p class="normal">Ah, Doolies's <a href="http://drjulieshow.com" title="singing career">singing career</a>. That's me in the front row (even though I missed Doolies's first concert in Taiwan last year). Doolies's joined the <a href="http://www.bluestreetvoices.com/index.html" title="Bluestreet Jazz Voices">Bluestreet Jazz Voices</a>, a Seattle jazz ensemble. It's a great chance for her to stretch her voice into a new genre.</p><p class="normal">We bought new pillows this weekend (the second time we've attempted to replace our current pillows, which date back further than I care to admit), and we went with extra firm. Doolies claims the first night I slept without a pillow. I don't remember. I plan to give them a few more days to see if they grow on me.</p>
