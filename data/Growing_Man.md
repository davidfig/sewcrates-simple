<p class="nospaceLeft">The inner man hides behind small comments and smaller entertainment.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">He grows only when others water and fertilize his fertile grounds.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">When left out of sunlight and out of reach of hand and mouth</p><p class="nospaceLeft">he must learn to grow as a fungus—without sunlight or tending.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">But perhaps that growth is more meaningful;</p><p class="nospaceLeft">feeding roots that can be reached through others tending.</p><br>
