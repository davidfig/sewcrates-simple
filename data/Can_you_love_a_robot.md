<p class="normal">Some may claim I stole robots from <a href="http://www.explodingdog.com/title/irememberyouusedtoloveme.html" title="Exploding Dog">Exploding Dog</a> (only one of many robot drawings), or more recently discovered <a href="http://www.wetherobots.com/2009/01/02/stomach-pain/" title="We the Robots">We the Robots</a> (they're all robots!). I won't say that they're wrong.</p><p class="normal">I've always had a fascination with robots. I wanted to build one as a child. The closest I got was realizing that microchips are made from silicon which is made from sand. Turning the sand into silicon and then microchips was a bit beyond my capabilities. I did take apart a Speak 'n Spell, thinking I could use it as the heart and voice of my robot. I instead ended up with a broken Speak 'n Spell.</p><p class="normal">It took me this long to realize how easy it was to create a robot with an <a href="http://www.wacom.com/cintiq/index.php" title="overpriced pen">overpriced pen</a>.</p>
