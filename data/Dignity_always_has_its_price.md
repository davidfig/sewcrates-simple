<p class="normal">A big pile of money! What could be better? Oh, yes, integrity. Don't worry, I didn't give up my integrity for the big pile of money. It just feels that way sometimes. It's my art dreams verses reality. Sometimes I get them confused as I don't truly know what's important in my life. The truth probably is that neither are important, and trying to find the meaning of importance is a fool's errand.</p><p class="normal">The dogs woke us up at 5:30am this morning. Not sure what they were barking at, but we had a rough morning. The week is drawing to a slow end with rainy nights and sunny evenings. We don't have much planned for the weekend. Hopefully that will change.</p>
