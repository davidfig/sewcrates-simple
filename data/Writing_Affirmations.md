<ol><li> Write something, write anything.<li> It doesn’t have to be good.<li> Make something happen in every story, page, and paragraph.<li> Cleverness is not the same as happenings.<li> Don’t worry about messages or themes, the story is the message and the themes.<li> Don’t edit until you have enough words finished. It’s like applying detail work to a painting before coating the canvas.<li> Make decisions and move the story forward.<li> Don’t worry about your decisions, the writing, or the story. It’s easy to change things.</ol>
