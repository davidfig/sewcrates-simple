<p class="nospaceLeft">Pulling back from the edges of daybreak</p><p class="nospaceLeft">avoiding talk of the next release</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">I am haunted by the causation of hope</p><p class="nospaceLeft">For Doolies has taught me how to couple</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">I pass the weight and fear its loss</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Learning only of beauty after beauty retreats</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Am I doomed to mistake and dwell alone?</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Feeling are far and walls thick to hide</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Cut, blood and missing pain I've now felt</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Tears-sorrow-loss-ache of receipt.</p><br>
