<p class="nospaceLeft">I want to tell of sadness.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">I want to rain tears.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">I want.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">I want to tell of sadness because.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">I want to rain tears because.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">I want.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">I want to tell of sadness because the telling allows me to relive.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">I want to rain tears because raining tears evokes.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">I want.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">I want to tell of sadness because the telling allows me to relive the memories that fall.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">I want to rain tears because raining tears evokes the squeezing pain.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">I want.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">I want to tell of sadness because the telling allows me to relive the memories that fall to the growing years.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">I want to rain tears because raining tears evokes the squeezing pains, the unquenched thrashing feelings.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">I want.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">I want to tell of sadness because the telling allows me to relive the memories that fall to the growing years.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">I want to rain tears because raining tears evokes the squeezing pain, the unquenched thrashing feelings.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">I want.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">I did sadness.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">I did raining tears.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">I want.</p><br>
