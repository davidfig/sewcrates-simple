<p class="normal">-Go on without me.</p><p class="normal">-Ok.</p><p class="normal">-No, really. Leave me here. I’m of no further use. It’s best off you go now.</p><p class="normal">-Got it.</p><p class="normal">-Where are you going?</p><p class="normal">-You said to go on without me.</p><p class="normal">-I didn’t mean for you to go on without me. I am suffering. How can you leave me alone like this?</p><p class="normal">-Oh.</p><p class="normal">-When I say go on without me, you’re supposed to sympathize. You stay behind and take care of me.</p><p class="normal">-Got it. I stay behind and take care of you.</p><p class="normal">-Right. Now, go on without me. I’m of no further use. I would only slow you down.</p><p class="normal">-Ok.</p><p class="normal">-You’re not getting it. I’m suffering here. Why would you leave me to suffer?</p><p class="normal">-Oh. So then you want me to stay?</p><p class="normal">-Isn’t that what I said?</p><p class="normal">-No, you said to go on without you.</p><p class="normal">-A real girlfriend would understand the difference between what I say and what I mean. When you told me you don’t like flowers, you didn’t really mean it, did you?</p><p class="normal">-But I never said I didn’t like flowers.</p><p class="normal">-Hypothetically, if you told me you didn’t like flowers, wouldn’t you still want me to buy you flowers?</p><p class="normal">-I like flowers. Why would I tell you not to buy them for me? You’re being ridiculous. Now, do you want me to wait here with you or leave?</p><p class="normal">-Do what you want. I’m a goner.
