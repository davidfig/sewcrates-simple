<p class="normal">When Button first arrived, I was a bit nervous: she was timid and didn't like Ziggy chasing her around and barking at her. Then ten minutes passed. They constantly play now, everywhere.</p><p class="normal">I didn't think anyone could keep up with Ziggy's energy, but Button hold her own. She ends up winning in most games: tug of war, chase the other dog around the couch at super high speeds, bite wrestle (where the winner gets a good nip at the ear), and many more we haven't been able to decipher yet. They slept together in the crate, and have learned some good (and not-so-good) habits from each other.</p><p class="normal">It's still too early to call it in the two-dog experiment, but it's looking good.</p>
