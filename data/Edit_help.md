<h3>Aligning</h3><p class="nospaceLeft">To align left, place -( as the first characters on that line.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">To align right, place -) as the first characters on that line.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">To center, place -- as the first characters on that line.</p><h3>Aligning with no paragraph spacing</h3><p class="nospaceLeft">To align left with no spacing, place /( as the first characters on the line.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">To align right with no spacing, place /) as the first characters on the line.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">To center with no spacing, place /- as the first characters on the line.</p><h3>Floating</h3><p class="nospaceLeft">To float left, place >( as the first characters on that line.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">To float right, place >) as the first characters on that line.</p><h3>Floating with no line spacing</h3><p class="nospaceLeft">To align left with no spacing, place <( as the first characters on the line.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">To align right with no spacing, place <) as the first characters on the line.</p><h3>Indenting</h3><p class="normal">Place a -> at the beginning of the line to indent the section.</p><h3>Line break</h3><p class="normal">Place a // at the beginning of the line to force a simple line break.</p><h3>Internal Links</h3><span>Use [title|/path/to/page</span><span>] to create a link.  The title| part is optional.  It will look like this: <a href="/Photos/" title="photographs">photographs</a>.</span><h3>External Links</h3><span>Use [title|http://path/to/page</span><span>] for external links.  The title| part is optional.  It will look like this: [http://msnbc.com].</span><h3>Pictures</h3><span>Use {picture URL|title|link URL</span><span>} to add a photo. The link URL part is optional.<h3>Headings</h3><p class="normal">To display a line as a heading, place as the first character on that line: ! for small headings, !! for medium headings, and !!! for large headings.</p><h3>Title and Subtitle</h3><p class="normal">To display a sewcrates.com heading, place a $ as the first character; for a sewcrates.com sub-heading, place $$ as the first characters.</p><h3>Emphasis</h3><p class="nospaceLeft">These can only be used for text on the same line.)</p><span>For <span class="bold">bold</span> use: #</span><span>#section_to_emphasize#</span><span>#</span><br><span>For <span class="italics">italics</span> use: '</span><span>'section_to_emphasize'</span><span>' (note there are two single quotes)</span><br><span>For <span class="underline">underline</span> use: _</span><span>_section_to_emphasize_</span><span>_ (note there are two underlines)</span><br><span>For <span class="smallcaps">smallcaps</span> use: +</span><span>+section_to_emphasize+</span><span>+</span><br><span>For strikethrough use: %</span><span>%section_to_emphasize%</span><span>%</span><h3>Bullet list</h3><p class="normal">Place a * at the beginning of the line to create a bullet list</p><h3>Numbered list</h3><p class="normal">Place a # at the beginning of the line to create a number list</p><h3>Private Section</h3><p class="normal">Place a  at the beginning and end of the section. It must be longer than one line (to get around the strikethrough).</p>
