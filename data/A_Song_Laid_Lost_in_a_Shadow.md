<p class="nospaceLeft">His children lay bare,</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Playing with their immitant friends.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">It wasn't fair</p><p class="nospaceLeft">His path was chosen, but</p><p class="nospaceLeft">He faught his hardest,</p><p class="nospaceLeft">His life closing before him.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">He wished it flashed,</p><p class="nospaceLeft">But instead it just longed.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Why wasn't he last? His life</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Just begun.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">The angels have gotten their wish,</p><p class="nospaceLeft">For it was time,</p><p class="nospaceLeft">They gathered their wits.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">The children got only half a deal,</p><p class="nospaceLeft">It wasn't fair, they should have</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Had more than just a decade, a decade</p><p class="nospaceLeft">To enjoy his life, but his song</p><p class="nospaceLeft">Laid lost in a shadow</p><p class="nospaceLeft">For love is lost and gained only</p><p class="nospaceLeft">In a memory</p><p class="nospaceLeft">A memory of our family love.</p><p class="nospaceLeft">A memory never truly lost.</p><br><p class="normal">Daddy. I love you.</p>
