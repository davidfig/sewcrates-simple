<p class="nospaceLeft">Hanging in a black oozing sky</p><p class="nospaceLeft">floats a brilliant point of night.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Over mountains shadowing blue</p><p class="nospaceLeft">glaring down battered sight.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Multitudes glance across its shine</p><p class="nospaceLeft">failing to notice its soft sigh.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">Yet few do stare and heed its call</p><p class="nospaceLeft">and follow to learn chains in time.</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">And when one does feel lessons learned,</p><p class="nospaceLeft">and accepts the untold truth</p><br><p class="nospaceLeft">then fate dost intercede a hand</p><p class="nospaceLeft">to impress being’s short turn.</p><br>
