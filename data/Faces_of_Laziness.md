<p class="normal">A new month is upon me again and I’m coming face-to-face with my laziness. I have an inexhaustible well of the stuff that I keep pulling from. Laziness is a horrible demon. After getting a taste of him, you crave more and more of the stuff until you’re drowning in it. My head is underwater lately and I’m grasping at anything to try yank myself up.</p><p class="normal">Another horrible aspect of laziness is that it’s easy to formulate plans to combat it when you’re not caught in its funk, and impossible to implement these plans when you are. Nature of the beast.</p><p class="normal">Moods are easily lost—the better or more productive the mood, the easier to lose it.</p>
