Just having her there kept me grounded and helped me through this difficult time.</p><p class="normal">Notice how in this Horrible, David is leaning on the Doolies. While I can't say I planned it that way, it is very appropriate.</p><b>Update</b>: Of course I planned to lean David on the Doolies. I don't know what I was thinking when I wrote that yesterday. It's even in the title. I guess when I look at these doodles three months later, much is loss as to my original inspiration. In some ways that's very good: I take a fresh look at it and see it from a different perspective. In other ways it's very bad: I completely forget what I was thinking when I first drew it.
